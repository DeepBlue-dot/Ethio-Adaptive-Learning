# 3D Geometry

Extending coordinate geometry to three dimensions allows us to model physical objects and space.

## Key Formulas:
*   **3D Distance**: $d = \sqrt{(x_2-x_1)^2 + (y_2-y_1)^2 + (z_2-z_1)^2}$
*   **Equation of a Plane**: $a(x-x_0) + b(y-y_0) + c(z-z_0) = 0$
*   **Vector Equation of a Line**: $\mathbf{r} = \mathbf{a} + t\mathbf{d}$
