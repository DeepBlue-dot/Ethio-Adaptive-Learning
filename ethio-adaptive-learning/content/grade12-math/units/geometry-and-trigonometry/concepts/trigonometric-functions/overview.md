# Trigonometric Functions

Trigonometry studies the relationships between side lengths and angles of triangles.

## Key Concepts:
*   **Unit Circle**: $x = \\cos\\theta$, $y = \\sin\\theta$.
*   **Identities**: $\\tan\\theta = \\frac{\\sin\\theta}{\\cos\\theta}$.
*   **Periodic Behavior**: Sine and cosine have a period of $2\\pi$.
