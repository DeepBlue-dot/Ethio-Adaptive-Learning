# Introduction to Descriptive Statistics

Statistics is the science of collecting, organizing, and interpreting data. Descriptive statistics focus on summarizing the data we have.

## Key Concepts:
*   **Mean**: The arithmetic average.
*   **Median**: The middle value when data is ordered.
*   **Mode**: The most frequent value.
*   **Standard Deviation**: A measure of how much the data deviates from the mean.
